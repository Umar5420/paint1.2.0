About Us

Established in 1981, Gobi’s Paints is a leading manufacturer in Pakistan that is dedicated to extend its development, production, distribution and service of world-class coating products in the UK. We use state-of-the-art European technology to provide pioneering, high quality and sustainable solutions to the public. We are distinguished for our excellent product quality, high professionalism, innovation and unparalleled customer service. Being experts in bespoke paint and coating variety, we offer a comprehensive portfolio of decorative and protective coats, industrial paints, auto refinish and construction chemicals that have set standards in the industry.

Allied Paint, a subsidiary of Gobi’s Paints is also devotedly working to deliver utmost performance and delicacy through its extensive range of award winning coating varieties. Today, Gobi’s Paints continues to serve millions of residential, commercial, and industrial sector customers with its high performing coating variety, specialty chemicals, and application systems. We take pride in our commitment to serving our customers with a heart warming experience.

Our Story

At Gobi’s Paints, we envision to transform vision into reality, and believe that colors have the power to transform lives; this is where our story began. Gobi’s Paints was established by Mr. Asif Malik in 1981, with the spirit to deliver premium quality coating products to customers in Pakistan. Our top brand features supreme quality and excellent customer service. Under the visionary leadership of Mr. Omar Asif Malik (CEO), Gobi’s Paints has become one of the leading coating manufacturers of Pakistan. With pride comes responsibility and earnest care, and since inception, we have been serving our customers nothing less than excellence. We strive to constantly innovate to exceed customer satisfaction; we embrace strong core values that reflect in its organizational culture. Our extensive range of coating products delivers high-quality and grace beyond imaginations. Today we are proud of how smartly we have contributed towards beautifying buildings and the strengthening coating industry.
